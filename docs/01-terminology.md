# Terminology

This document defines key terms used in the TOR specification.

---

## Terms of Reference (TOR)

A document that defines the objectives, scope, deliverables,
and conditions of a project or procurement.

---

## Requirement

A clearly defined expectation or condition that must be satisfied.

---

## Deliverable

A measurable output that must be produced as part of the project.

---

## Scope of Work

The set of activities required to achieve the objectives of the project.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

Conditions that must be met for deliverables to be accepted.
