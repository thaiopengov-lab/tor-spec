# Conformance

This document describes what qualifies as a TOR that conforms
to this specification.

---

## Minimum Requirements

A TOR document is considered compliant with **TOR Specification v0.1**
if it includes the following sections:

- Background
- Objectives
- Scope of Work
- Deliverables
- Timeline
- Acceptance Criteria

---

## Optional Extensions

Additional sections may be included depending on context.

Examples include:

- Budget
- Vendor Qualification
- Evaluation Criteria
